Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

‚ñº

Yes! Tap Tap Track is designed with privacy as the core principle. All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The app operates offline by default. If you enable optional location logging, only anonymous coordinates are sent directly to Apple's servers for business and location name search. Tap Tap Track does not collect, store, or have access to any location data. We don't collect any information, track usage, or use any analytics services. Your tracked events and personal data remain private and stored locally.

Do I need to create an account?

‚ñº

No account is required! Download the app and start tracking immediately. There's no sign-up, no email, no phone number needed. Just download and use.

How do I create custom categories and events?

‚ñº

Go to the Manage tab and tap "Add" next to Categories or Event Presets. For categories, just enter a name. For event presets, you can customize:

  • Name: Choose any name for your event
  • Icon: Select from 200+ icons across 12 categories
  • Color: Pick from 16 color options
  • Category: Assign to any category you've created

Can I edit or delete events after tracking them?

‚ñº

Yes! Go to the History tab, find the event you want to edit, and tap the edit icon. You can change the time, date, add or modify notes, or delete the event entirely.

How do I track an event that happened in the past?

‚ñº

Long-press the event button instead of just tapping it. This will log the event and immediately open the edit screen where you can change the date and time to when it actually occurred.

Can I export my data?

‚ñº

Yes! Go to the History tab and use the export feature to download all your tracked events as a CSV file. This is perfect for backups or analyzing your data in spreadsheet applications.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

‚ñº

If you uninstall the app, all data stored locally on your device will be deleted. However, if you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iOS settings, the data may be included in your device backup. To be safe, export your data to CSV before uninstalling if you want to keep it.

Can I sync my data across multiple devices?

‚ñº

Tap Tap Track stores all data locally on each device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your data may be included in your device backups, but there's no automatic syncing between devices. You can export your data from one device and import it manually if needed.

What iOS version do I need?

‚ñº

Tap Tap Track requires iOS 17.0 or later. This ensures you have access to the latest SwiftData features and the best performance.

How many events can I track?

‚ñº

There's no limit! You can track as many events as you want. The app uses efficient local storage, so you're only limited by your device's storage capacity.

Can I delete a category or preset?

‚ñº

Yes! In the Manage tab, you can delete categories or event presets. When deleting a preset, you'll have the option to delete just the preset (keeping the tracked events) or delete both the preset and all associated events.

  • Work: Meetings, calls, tasks completed
