The Short Version

Tap Tap Track collects zero data. All your information is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server. We don't know who you are, what you track, or how you use the app. That's by design.

Introduction

Tap Tap Track ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by Time Lapse Technologies LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Tap Tap Track mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This commitment is reflected in our core design philosophy: we built Tap Tap Track to function entirely offline with no data collection whatsoever.

Information We Collect

We collect no information.

Tap Tap Track does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or any other data to our servers or any third-party servers. Specifically:

  • No personal information â€” We don't ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • No usage analytics â€” We don't track how you use the app, which features you access, or how often you open the app.

  • No crash reports â€” We don't collect or receive crash reports or error logs.

Data Storage

All data you create in Tap Tap Track is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Tracked events and their timestamps
  • Notes and context you add to events
  • Categories you create
  • Event presets and their configurations

This data:

  • Remains on your device at all times
  • Is automatically backed up by iOS if you use iCloud Backup (controlled by your iOS settings, not by us)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be exported by you at any time via the CSV export feature

Network Access

Tap Tap Track does not connect to the internet.

The app has no network capabilities. It cannot and does not make any network requests. Your data physically cannot leave your device through our app because the app has no ability to transmit data.

Third-Party Services

Tap Tap Track does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data processors. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics service
  • Crash reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Any third-party frameworks that collect data

Data Export

Tap Tap Track includes a CSV export feature that allows you to export all your tracked events. When you use this feature:

  • The export is generated locally on your device
  • You control where the exported file is saved or shared
  • We never see or have access to your exported data

Children's Privacy

Tap Tap Track does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, there are no special provisions necessary for children's privacy beyond our general policy of zero data collection.

Data Security

Your data is protected by the security measures built into your iOS device, including:

  • Device encryption
  • Face ID / Touch ID protection
  • Passcode lock
  • App sandboxing (other apps cannot access Tap Tap Track's data)

Since your data never leaves your device, there is no risk of server breaches or data leaks from our end â€” we have no servers and hold no data.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect any data, traditional data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) are exercised entirely through your control of your own device:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app
  • Export: Use the CSV export feature anytime
  • Deletion: Delete individual events in the app, or uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Correction: Edit any event directly in the app

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. However, our commitment to zero data collection is fundamental to Tap Tap Track and will not change.
